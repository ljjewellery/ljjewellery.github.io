About Lesley Jane Jewellery

Making beautiful things from sustainably sourced metal

I work predominantly in copper, using reclaimed or recycled materials wherever possible. Some of my work is enamelled and I also do some small-scale coppersmithing and sometimes recycled silver, when I feel like a change! Previously based at Flameworks Creative Arts Facility, in Plymouth, I moved to a shipping container studio in Cardiff in 2017. My lovely little shipping container gave me enough room to start teaching small group beginners jewellery making workshops but, as the teaching side of my business has grown, I found that Id outgrown that space and so, as of June 2019, my business is based in a warehouse style building at The Bridge Studios, on Western Avenue in Cardiff.
