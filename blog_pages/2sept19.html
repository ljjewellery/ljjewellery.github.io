Latest News

Now that I've settled in to my new work space, I'll be holding an open studio as part of the Bridge Studios Saturday Social on 21st September

There will be jewellery and metalworking demonstrations throughout the day:

Using reclaimed metal
Piercing saw demonstration
Torch-fired enamelling
Raising a copper bowl

Free bangle making activity, suitable for adults and accompanied children. One bangle per person, while stocks of the blanks (pre-made by me) last!

It's Bridge Studios Saturday Social day, so there will be Craft Ales by out own onsite microbrewery, Arcadian Brewing, plus street food made on site as well.

Fellow maker Faye from Ether Silver Home will also be opening the doors of her portacabin studio and there will be guest makers and traders in the communal space onsite.
